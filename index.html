About Us

We are a small team of experienced, dedicated women who believe that clothing is more than just something to put between you and the weather. We want to make your clothes an expression of your own irreplaceable beauty, and make sure they remain that way for as long as you need them to. We have over 15 years of combined professional experience in making and repairing apparell of all kinds, and we especially love to help women make their wedding day something to be proud of forever. We don't care if "the clothes make the man" -- we're the women who make the best clothes!
